Dear Shubhi,

A Little Voice Note For You 🎙️

You are the most beautiful part of every day. Your smile has the power to light up the darkest rooms, and your heart is as pure as magic. ✨

I hope this special 23rd birthday brings you as much absolute happiness as you bring to everyone around you. Keep shining, keep smiling, and never stop being the wonderful, aesthetic, and gorgeous person you are! 💖

An Important Note...

"Apni feelings kaise express karun tumhare saamne, mujhe kuch samajh nahi aa raha hai... par koshish kar raha hoon, shayad tum samajh pao.

Jo main aaj keh raha hoon, wo dil mein kaafi waqt se tha. Sochta raha ki bolun ya nahi... par jo cheez dil se nikle, usse andar daba kar rakhna itna asaan bhi nahi hota. Toh aaj bas bol deta hoon — sirf isliye ki dil halka ho jaye." Main chahta toh yeh sab tumhe call pe bhi bata sakta tha, par jaanta hoon main baat karte karte shayad bohot emotional ho jata... aur main bilkul nahi chahta tha ki meri wajah se tumhe apne upar zara sa bhi pressure feel ho. Isliye aaj sab likh kar bata raha hoon. ❤️

Tum sach mein mere liye bohot important ho, aur hamesha rahogi. Mujhe aaj bhi wo pehla din yaad hai jab hum mile the... tumne badi masoomiyat se poocha tha, "Park kiske saath aate ho?" aur jab maine kaha "Akele"... toh tumne ek chhoti si muskaan ke saath kaha tha, "Ab se main aaya karungi aapke saath."

Shayad tumhe yaad bhi nahi hoga, par un chhote chhote lamhon ne mujhe ek ajeeb sa sukoon diya tha. Pata nahi kab tum mere din ka sabse pyaara hissa ban gayi. Bhagwan ji ne milaya hai toh kuch socha hi hoga, nahi milna hota toh shayad aisa coincidence hi nahi hota ki humari baat hoti.

Tum aksar poochti ho na, ki "Tumhe main hi kyun pasand hoon?" Toh sach bataun? Mere paas iska koi ek seedha sa jawab nahi hai... kyunki tumhari kisi ek baat pe dil nahi aaya mera. Tumhari bholi si wo hassi jismein saari duniya ki khushi chhupi lagti hai, tumhara sabki itni fikr karna, aur tumhari wo masoomiyat jo bina kuch kahe hi sab kuch keh jaati hai. Maine tumhe nahi chuna, balki mere dil ne us sukoon ko chuna jo sirf aur sirf tumhare aas-paas milta hai. Tum 'tum' ho, aur bas isiliye tum meri sabse pyaari khwaish ban gayi ho. ✨

Suno, guzre waqt ne mujhse bahut kuch chheena hai, aur aane wale waqt mein bhi shayad bahut kuch khone wala hoon... par sach kahun, main tumhe nahi khona chahta. Main nahi chahta ki tum aur main juda ho jayein, ya ek dusre ke samne ajnabi ban jayein. Humare beech koi aisi doori aaye jise hum chah kar bhi khatm na kar payein, aur tumhe meri wajah se koi bhi dukh mile... aisa main kabhi nahi hone dena chahta. Main duniya ka sab kuch khone ko taiyaar hoon, magar tumhe nahi...

Khair... shayad yeh mere fizool khayal hain, kyunki mujhe lagne laga hai ki tumne mann bana liya hai mujhse door jaane ka. Par sach yahi hai ki mera yeh dil sach mein nahi chahta ki tumhe meri zindagi se door jaana pade.

Yeh sab keh kar main tum par koi umeed ya demand nahi thop raha. Main bas yeh isliye bata raha hoon taaki mujhe kabhi is baat ka regret na rahe ki maine tumhe apne dil ki baat hi nahi batayi. Meri feelings tum tak pahunchani thi, toh pahunch gayi... aage sach mein jo bhi tumhari marzi hogi, main uski poori izzat karunga. Par agar mere dil ki suno toh... main bas yahi chahta hoon ki tum hamesha mere saath raho. 🌸

Suno, main jaanta hoon ki abhi aana tabiyat ki wajah se mushkil hai, aur main bilkul nahi chahta ki tum dard mein aao. Tum pehle apna achhe se dhyan rakho, aaram karo aur theek ho jao. Jab tum bilkul theek aur comfortable feel karo, tab hum milenge. Par... milna zaroori hai! 😊 Main theek usi jagah par milunga jahan sabse pehli baar humari woh ek bohot pyaari si baat hui thi...
